# Everyday Habits to Help You Lose Weight and Get in Shape ...

Getting in shape, losing weight and feeling great is all about creating a healthier lifestyle and with this you may develop some rituals you follow on a daily basis. Rituals like using your legs as transportation when you can instead of jumping in the car at every opportunity can make a difference. As can choosing to graze instead of gorge. One more thing, make sure you exercise on a regular basis with no excuses. These rituals will help you to slim down and get in your best shape!

## 1. Keep a Glass of Water Next to You

![Keep a Glass of Water Next to You](https://img.allw.mn/content/kg/x5/fyvfeb5256f318ef13d01898235540_480x270.gif)

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated, eliminate toxins and get rid of the bloat. If you keep a glass of water next to you at all times you will be reminded to drink. And you will not be drinking water later in the day and interrupting sleep with unexpected bathroom breaks. So drink to your health, weight and aiding your digestive system.

## 2. Do Not Skip Meals

![Do Not Skip Meals](https://img.allw.mn/content/ma/sw/bft86v2456f31b91805b2546404166_500x300.gif)

Skipping meals will cause you to have mood swings, slow down your metabolism and feel all out of sorts. So stop skipping meals and then eating like a line-backer later. If you eat mini meals you will have more energy and never feel hungry again. And the bonus is that you will boost your metabolism!

## 3. Jog Wherever You Go

![Jog Wherever You Go](https://img.allw.mn/content/nh/rp/b0hhbenv56f31b506c0f8482236011_500x200.gif)

The two block walk to your car can be a total waste of possible energy expended, so push yourself and jog to your car. I often race my kids to the car and I make every possible fitness moment matter. Small changes add up to make a serious difference. So go ahead and make every moment matter by developing healthy fitness rituals each day!

## 4. Schedule Your Workouts Everyday

![Schedule Your Workouts Everyday](https://img.allw.mn/content/vo/ox/n9ojw3wd56f31c8e03d2c502890690_500x209.gif)

How many times do you skip your workout because you are tired, busy or just not into it? Hopefully your reply was never but if you are like most, this does happen. So change your daily lifestyle and schedule your workouts to be nonnegotiable. This means no matter how busy, tired, bored or not that into it you are, you still get it done!

## 5. Eat Your Greens Daily

![Eat Your Greens Daily](https://img.allw.mn/content/sh/v3/kkse5x8h56f31f38a815b407352460_280x217.gif)

Add some green into your life to increase your fiber, minerals and vitamins. You will speed up your digestive tract, increase your metabolism and also just feel so much better. Make this part of your daily plan and you will see amazing results. Life is what you make of it so make today a better day than yesterday!

## 6. Graze but Don’t Gorge

![Graze but Don’t Gorge](https://img.allw.mn/content/km/kk/qp1wg0pp56f3239abfc0b448816866_480x270.gif)

Graze at meal times rather than gorge. Eat on a salad plate because dinner plates are way too big. If you stick to portion control regularly your body will have less to digest as a time, causing your metabolic rate to fly high. And you will begin to see amazing results in your body so make this part of your daily ritual!

## 7. Get to Bed Earlier

![Get to Bed Earlier](https://img.allw.mn/content/xs/ez/wzotnnfp56f324e0797bb106518592_500x253.gif)

Getting to bed earlier is not always easy, especially if you have a busy life that seems to go nonstop all day. But getting to bed earlier will allow you time to catch plenty of zzzs and your body to recover from the day. If you want your body to perform at its best, sleep is ultra-important. So aim for 7-9 hours of sleep every night!

## 8. Don't Snack at Night

![performance, stage, singing, music, nightclub,](https://img.allw.mn/content/qc/v6/btrmox8d58cffd8366526780272435_460x248.gif)

We all get tempted to plop down in bed or on the couch with a bag of chips or popcorn after a long day-don't. Late night snacking causes your body to poorly digest and use the food which in turn causes weight gain.

## 9. Laughter is the Best Medicine

![,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xs/ns/epaw85aj58cffce0366e7996452352_500x245.gif)

When it comes to our lives, it seems stress rules it all. Find something you enjoy or a friend to have a daily laugh chat with. Not only does it decrease levels of cortisol in the body which actually promotes weight gain, but studies on laughter have shown that one hour of intense laughter burns 120 calories.

## 10. Treat Snacks as Meals

![hair, person, beauty, hairstyle, brown hair,](https://img.allw.mn/content/rl/hr/qgd0l3al58cffd2887eb5164042461_400x226.gif)

Every diet plan shows eating 5 small meals a day. This is key to weight loss because it makes sure your body has a constant source of food coming in for energy, preventing the body from storing it like when it goes in starvation mode. Snacks may not seem like a big deal, but they add up to high calories when you aren't eating the right stuff. Treat your snacks as mini meals and make sure you're fueling your body in between main meals to help lose weight.

With these everyday rituals you will slim down, shape up and feel incredible. Life is all about choices so choose to better your health each and every day! I promise you will be glad that you did!
