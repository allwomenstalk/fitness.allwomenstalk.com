# Workouts to Motivate Girls Who Are Tired to Work out ...

it's been a long day and you are so tired the last thing you want to do is work out. However exercise can help boost your energy and improve your health so it is ultra-important to know workouts for when you are tired. And if you are tired and you push yourself to work out, it is pretty much a guarantee that you will have loads more energy when you finish. So get up from the couch to get your workout on. You are only as strong as your last workout so give it all you got!

## 1. Weights While You Watch TV

Too tired to get up for a run? No worries because you can get a great weighted workout without having to push hard. Just grab a light set of weights and perform exercises like in this video while tuning in to your favorite show. The surge of energy you may feel may motivate you to turn off the television and increase your workout intensity!

## 2. Pilates

You have always wanted to try the Pilates class in your gym but by the time you get off of work you are just way too tired. Well wait no longer because you can follow this video and workout in your living room. No need to drive to the gym. Get motivated in the comfort of your home starting today!

## 3. Stretch It out

From sitting at work all day, your body just feels tight and you really could use a good stretch. Stretch it out to relieve your joints of stress. After your stretch you may feel so amazing you will want to increase the intensity and add a cardio routine. Or this may prepare you for a sleep filled night!

## 4. Swim It out

Nothing like pool water to wake you up, especially if you had a really long day! Awaken your senses and open your mind to a cool water workout, like in this video. It is a super effective way to wake up and work out!

## 5. Yoga

Anytime and anywhere you can perform this yoga workout to wake you up and help you achieve a better body. Fitness is not just about looking good but you will also feel better after this super workout. So what are you waiting for? Follow along to get fit and feel awesome!

## 6. Walking

Walk it out to rid yourself of stress and wake your body up. Walking is a great way to burn calories without the high intensity stress of other activities. And if you feel up to the challenge, increase the intensity by walking a hilly route. An incline will help you to burn more calories and get in better shape sooner!

## 7. Biking

Burn fat, get fit and feel fab with a biking workout. You can follow along and do this on your stationary bike at home or venture to the great outdoors. Whatever you do, make sure you push yourself so you can get a really great workout. Like is what you make of it so give it all you got!

So wakey wakey because all of these workouts will help you to push through your sleepy state and get in your best shape. As long as you are ready for the challenge? So are you ready?
