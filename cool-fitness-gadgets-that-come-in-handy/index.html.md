# 7 Cool Fitness Gadgets That Come in Handy ...

I must admit that I love buying gadgets of any kind, but lately I've really enjoyed looking for fitness gadgets. Many of the gadgets that I've found can be very useful during workouts. Some of them even come in handy at other times. If you like gadgets as much as I do, here's 7 fitness gadgets for you to check out.

## 1. HAPIfork

![HAPIfork](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ua/o7/0pkl5dp4_450x433.jpg)

The HAPIfork is one of the neatest fitness gadgets that I've ever seen. It is a fork that will monitor how fast you're eating. Many studies show that eating too fast can lead to weight gain. The HAPIfork is designed to help you eat slower by lighting up and vibrating if you're eating too fast. It is also available in five different colors.

## 2. Fitbit One

![Fitbit One](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/09/zp/hddd4frv_600x399.jpg)

There are several different gadgets that you can get that are made by Fitbit, but this one seems to have it all. The Fitbit One tracks a variety of your activities, such as steps taken and calories burned. If you wear the Fitbit One on your arm while you sleep, it will track how many hours you slept and how much you woke up during that time. You can also export the data from your Fitbit One to the fitness app that you currently use.

## 3. Body Media Fit Core

![Body Media Fit Core](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sx/yg/mfnu7bgy_600x385.jpg)

The Body Media Fit Core has been used by many of the contestants on 'The Biggest Loser.' This gadget can calculate how many calories you've burned, track the hours you sleep at night, and count the number of steps you take each day. You can easily manage your data online, as well. The Body Media Fit Core is compatible with many fitness apps like MyFitnessPal and SparkPeople.

## 4. Fitbit Aria Wi-Fi Smart Scale

![Fitbit Aria Wi-Fi Smart Scale](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ys/zq/l1v2w6zz_600x415.jpg)

The Fitbit Aria Wi-Fi Smart Scale does much more than a typical scale does. After it calculates your weight and body fat percentage, it automatically uploads the results to your Fitbit account. The scale can be used by up to 8 different people, but you won't have to worry about the other users seeing your results because all of your information is kept private. If you have a weight loss goal that you're trying to achieve, this is a great gadget to have.

## 5. Up 24 by Jawbone

![Up 24 by Jawbone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c2/eg/m7gtckik_600x263.jpg)

The Up 24 wristband is a great fitness gadget that stays connected to your smartphone. This allows you to get real-time updates on your progress. The Up 24 wristband is available in 5 different colors. It also has up to 7 days of battery life after being charged for about 2 hours. It's made of hypoallergenic rubber, which is splash resistant. It can also be used to track your sleep patterns, including how long you sleep, how long it takes you to fall asleep, and how often you wake up. The wristband can also be used to track the amount of time you are active and the amount of time that you're inactive. It even has a smart alarm, which can be used to wake you up.

## 6. Motorola MOTOACTV

![Motorola MOTOACTV](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/eh/egg8zqci_253x184.jpg)

The Motorola MOTOACTV is an all-in-one device. It has an mp3 player that measures your performance against the songs on your playlist. This allows it to create a playlist that will help you maximize your performance. It also has a heart rate monitor and a GPS fitness tracker. The MOTOACTV is one of the toughest fitness gadgets around. It is rain resistant and sweat proof. It comes with a wrist strap, but it can be clipped to a belt or your clothing, too.

## 7. Finis Neptune Underwater MP3 Player

![Finis Neptune Underwater MP3 Player](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wz/cw/ymxrt5jc_300x88.jpg)

I love gadgets that can be used in water, such as the Finis Neptune Underwater mp3 player. It is waterproof and can provide clear sounds in water that is up to 10 feet deep. It is also very easy to use and has 4 GB of storage, which will allow you to store about 60 hours of playback. It does support most audio formats and is compatible with iTunes. One of the best things about the Finis Neptune is that it fits very securely on your goggles, so you won't have to worry about it coming off in the water.

Fitness gadgets are a great addition to any workout plan. Do you currently use any fitness gadgets that you would like to recommend to me? I can't wait to hear from you.
