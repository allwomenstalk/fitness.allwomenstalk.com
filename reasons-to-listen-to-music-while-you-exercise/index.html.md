# 7 Reasons to Listen to Music While You Exercise ...

Did you know that there are many reasons to listen to music when you exercise? Many people feel that music helps them make it through difficult workouts. If you don’t listen to music when you exercise I highly recommend trying it. Be sure to check out these reasons to listen to music when you exercise too. You will quickly see that music can have a positive effect on your workouts.

## 1. Distraction

One of the biggest reasons to listen to music when you exercise is that it can provide you with a distraction. Doing the same exercises every day can get boring. If you’re tired of your current routine, try listening to music. Hearing some of your favorite songs can provide you with a much needed distraction.

## 2. Improve Your Mood

Everyone has days when they are in a terrible mood. If you’re having a bad day, it can be very tempting to skip your workout. However, if you push yourself to exercise anyway, you will be happy that you did. Music has a way of putting people in a good mood and making them feel great. If you're struggling with a situation that is going on in your life, music can even change your perspective and make you look at things differently.

## 3. No Workout Buddy Needed

Do you enjoy working out with a friend? Having a workout buddy is a lot of fun. Unfortunately, it can often be difficult to find a time that coordinates with both of your schedules. Some people even lose their motivation to exercise when they don’t have someone to exercise with. However, if you listen to music while you exercise you won’t be alone. You will get to spend some time with your favorite singers and sing along to some of your favorite songs.

## 4. Discover New Music

If you exercise while listening to music, be sure to create a workout playlist. Creating a playlist can be a lot of fun. Be sure to add a variety of uplifting songs to the line up. Upbeat tunes will motivate you to push yourself harder as you work out. If you add some songs that you normally wouldn't listen to, you just might discover some new favorites. Be sure to add new songs to your playlist often so you don’t get bored with it.

## 5. Something to Look Forward to

We all have things that we look forward to. Some people look forward to their daily workouts and that is wonderful. Unfortunately, I’m not one of those people. However, I am a music lover and always look forward to listening to music when I can. I must admit that lately I've been looking forward to my workouts a little more because I know that I’ll get to listen to music.

## 6. Improves Your Focus

If you've got a lot on your mind, it can be difficult to concentrate on your workout. It may even make you consider skipping it. It can also be difficult to focus on your workout when other people are around you. Listening to music can eliminate outside noise and help you concentrate on your workout.

## 7. Improves Your Performance

There are days when it can be quite tempting to do a bare minimum workout. Listening to music will keep you from doing that. Instead of exercising at a slower pace than you should, music will make you feel like giving it your all. You will be thrilled by how successful your workout is.

As you can see there are many benefits of listening to music when you exercise. Do you like to exercise with or without music? I can’t wait to hear what you think.
