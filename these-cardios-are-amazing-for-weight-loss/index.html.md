# These Cardios Are Amazing for Weight Loss ...

I confess: I used to loathe cardio. But a few years ago, I discovered it doesn't have to be a chore, and in fact, it can even be - gasp! - fun. So whether you're looking for a new cardio exercise to help you lose weight, stay fit, or just have more energy, here are the best cardios for weight loss, fitness, and energy (see what I did there)?

![vacation,sea,walking,adventure,physical fitness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/su/sf8yk_vacation_sea_walking_adventure_physical-fitness_500x500.jpg)

Source: [Bff's](https://weheartit.com/entry/113380692/search?context_type=search&context_user=_littleeprincess&page=5&query=trail+running)
So it doesn't look like much - running on a trail - but even without much of an elevation change, trail running is so much more of a cardio workout than even regular running. OMG!

![Downtown,blue,image,architecture,skyscraper,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b3/to/gp816_downtown_blue_image_architecture_skyscraper_500x500.jpg)

![clothing,vacation,muscle,leg,swimwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/rl/on5ds_clothing_vacation_muscle_leg_swimwear_500x500.jpg)

## 9. Swimming

![swimming,underwater,swimming pool,sports,water sport,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/ye/xnh99_swimming_underwater_swimming-pool_sports_water-sport_500x500.jpg)

Source: [| We Heart It](https://weheartit.com/entry/175919819/search?context_type=search&context_user=lifestyle_for_you&query=swimming)
If your joints can't handle a weight-bearing workout (like running), then swimming is for you. It's still a killer cardio workout, but it's gentler on your joints.
