# Tips to Motivate You to Get off the Couch to Exercise ...

Looking for some **tips to motivate you to get off the couch?** Do some soul searching and ask yourself what you are accomplishing by sitting on the couch, eating chips, drinking Coke and watching soaps? Chances are you're not feeling as healthy as you could. Limiting your couch time can help you look and feel fitter. So here are some **tips to motivate you to get off the couch.**

## 1. Make a Room in Your House Totally Fitness-oriented

Have a television and DVD player with a ton of exercise DVDs, motivaton posters, fitness books and magazines, water bottles, boom box to blast music, etc. Creating a room without a place to sit down and relax is one of the best **tips to motivate you to get off the couch.**

## 2. Choose Exercises That You Actually like

If you get bored walking, do weights. If you hate weights, do an aerobics bike. If you hate an aerobics bike, then walk. The point is to find what you like so you're more likely to stick with it.

## 3. Challenge Yourself to Just 10 Minutes

After a while, you'll be so pumped up that you will want to keep going!

## 4. Make Exercise Fun!

Go shopping for cute exercise clothes. Treat yourself to a new exercise DVD. Buy yourself a new pedometer. Fun exercise is more likely to become a part of your routine.

## 5. Workout with a Friend

She will be a healthy distraction from an otherwise lonely and boring workout.

## 6. Mix It up

Go online, especially on YouTube, then pick and choose any workout you want. Workouts may be found for any age or fitness level.

## 7. Make a Plan

When you know it is time for the soaps to come on, shut off the tube and start your exercise.

## 8. Reward Yourself

Include a cheat day and have your favorite dish. This will keep you from feeling deprived.

## 9. Clean and Organize Your House

Run the sweeper, polish the furniture, do the dishes, do the laundry, etc. Housework is activity too and has to be done anyways!

## 10. Make It a Habit

Exercise any time of the day that you want to, but do it! You are more likely to stick with it if it is the time of day you are most motivated.

What good are you going to be for your loved ones if you get obese with health problems? You have only been given one body. Now get up!
