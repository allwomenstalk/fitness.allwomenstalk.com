# Lets Get Physical : 5 Best YouTube Channels for Workout Videos ...

What are the **best YouTube channels for workout videos?**

Who doesn't love YouTube? You can watch everything from funny cat videos to makeup tutorials, but have you ever thought of using it to up your fitness game?

You don't need a gym membership, all you need is an internet connection to feel the burn. Here are the **best YouTube channels for workout videos.**

## 1. POPSUGAR Fitness

PopSugar Fitness has a lot of different [videos to watch](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/must-watch-youtube-videos-for-when-youre-bored/), from full-body workouts to 30-minute workouts. The 30-minute workouts are perfect for those who don't have enough time. The no-equipment cardio workout playlist is another must. There is even a playlist for beginners too. PopSugar Fitness is definitely one of the **best YouTube channels for workout videos.**

## 2. Yoga with Adriene

Yoga with Adriene is definitely one of the best channels to watch for those who are either starting out have been doing yoga for years. She recently added a video "Yoga for Weight Loss" that is only 17 minutes long! Another plus is she has a lot of videos for anxiety and stress too. So if you need to unwind after work, just turn on YouTube and follow Adriene's lead.

## 3. BeFit

BeFit is a fun channel that has a large variety of exercises. You can find free videos from Denise Austin, one of the most famous fitness instructors there is. She has a playlist on the channel that has a workout for the office too. Also, if you love to dance, there are videos for you too.

## 4. Women's Health

For those who didn't know, Women's Health magazine has an official YouTube channel! The channel has playlists about health and wellness tips, beauty tips and tricks, quick workouts, fitness tips, yoga, and sex and relationships. So there is a lot going on, including helping you get in shape.

## 5. XHIT Daily

XHIT Daily has some of the best intense workouts ever. Don't worry, they do have a beginners playlist too. So you'll find on the channel the 7-Day Workout Plan, the Little Black Dress Workouts, along with celebrity-inspired workouts and Victoria Secret Models. By the way, Rebecca Louise is one of the best instructors!

So good luck on finding the best workout for you!
