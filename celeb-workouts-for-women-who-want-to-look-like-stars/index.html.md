# 10 Celeb Inspired Workouts to Look like the Stars ...

You don't have to be famous in order to look fabulous. Of course, stars do have some secrets that we should steal. Here are a few celebrity workouts for women who want to look like stars:

## 1. Julianne Hough's Dancing Workout

Dancers have the best bodies. That's why you should add some dance moves into your routine, and there's no one better to take dancing advice from than Julianne Hough. After all, she's one of the biggest names from Dancing With the Stars. If you watch this video, you'll learn how to move your hips and legs easier. Then you won't be afraid to walk around in shorts and a crop top like she does.

## 2. Kim Kardashian Butt Workout

If you're not a fan of your flat booty, then you can try the workout in this video. It'll help tone your butt, so that you can wear your skintight dresses with confidence. You're going to start by doing pulses and then move on to some exercises that are a little more intense. If you do this workout often enough, then you'll feel better about your booty than you ever have before.

## 3. Jessica Alba Crossfit Workout

You've heard about CrossFit before. It's pretty intense, but the moves in this video doesn't require any equipment, so you might as well try it. It can't hurt! If you're a beginner, just stick to watching the woman on the right, because she'll show you how to do the easiest version of each workout. Once you're able to handle level one, you can move on to following the woman on the middle, and then the woman on the left.

## 4. Selena Gomez Ab Workout

Selena is more than just an amazing singer. She's also incredibly gorgeous as well. If you want to get abs like she has, then you should follow the advice in this video. You're going to start out by knee dancing. It sounds silly, but it's actually a pretty great move. Watch and see for yourself!

## 5. Heidi Klum Workout

This video consists entirely of squats. Of course, they're all different variations. Switching things up is how Heidi Klum gets such a gorgeous body! You're going to start by doing squats for a full minute. Then you're going to be doing tap and squats for a full minute. After that, you'll learn about even more squats that your body will benefit from.

## 6. How to Get a Body like JLo

It doesn't matter how old JLo is. She still has a sexy body. That's why you should try doing the moves that she does on a daily basis. The workout in this video is perfect for women who want to tone their legs. Skip to 1:00 if you'd like to see the workout in action.

## 7. Taylor Swift's Workout

This is a full body workout that's going to take you twenty minutes to do. It's performed by the woman who shows Taylor Swift, Julianne Hough, and Reese Witherspoon how to workout their bodies. By the time you're finished, you'll probably be worn out. Of course, you'll also be super proud of yourself.

## 8. Jennifer Aniston's Workout for "We're the Millers"

She looked pretty great in this movie, didn't she? If you want that body, follow this workout plan and you should be looking as toned and svelte as Jennifer in no time at all. It won't be easy, but it will be totally worth it.

## 9. Khloe Kardashian Workout Routine

Kim isn't the only Kardashian that looks great. Her sister is looking pretty toned and fabulous these days too. That's thanks in part to this workout. It targets all those body parts you need to look hot, including your butt, abs and legs.

## 10. Priyanka Chopra's Shocking Workout Routine

Here's a celebrity who is an inspiration to all of us. She's realistic about how she eats and wants us all to know that she works hard for her body. You can take a cue from her and do the same by enjoying your food, but exercising to stay in shape. This workout is a great place to start.
