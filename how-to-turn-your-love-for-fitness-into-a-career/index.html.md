# How to Turn Your Love for Fitness into a Career ...

Are you someone who is so into health and fitness that it takes up the majority of your time, energy, and thought process? If your entire life revolves around your fitness journey and your love of fitness, then why not turn it into something that can benefit you financially as well as physically and emotionally? There are more opportunities now than ever to do so. Here is **how to turn your love for fitness into a career**.

## 1. Fitness Blog

Start putting time into your passion by developing a fitness blog. You can share all of your tips and tricks, as well as tutorials and tracking your own progress, and if you have a knack for spreading the message, you will find that the followers and subscribers start to come organically. The more you post and the more your share around the internet, the more hits you will get.

## 2. Business Skills

To make sure that you go in to your new project with the best chance of success, it can really help to brush up and hone in on your business knowledge before you begin. There are plenty of courses and online seminars that you can be a part of that will give you lots of important information with regards to starting your own business. It’s especially useful if you have never been self employed before.

## 3. Internships

Search around your local area for any internships in the fitness industry that you might be able to apply for. There might be slots in gyms, or places in independent companies that are looking to train and hire personal trainers. If you would prefer to be part of a team rather than creating your own brand and going it alone, this can be a really good road to go down.

## 4. Personal Training

You’ve got all of the equipment, you know all of the best routines, so why not just put yourself out there and advertise your services as a freelance personal trainer? You can start with friends and family, and then once you have a number of good testimonials you can branch out in to the wider public. Thank to social media, it is easier to advertise your own small business than ever before.
