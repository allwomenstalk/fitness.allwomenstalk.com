# The Best Websites for Buying Affordable Yoga Pants ...

Yoga pants are becoming pretty popular, which is why you should have a few pairs tucked away in your drawers. Of course, you don't want to spend a ridiculous amount of money on them when you can get something similar for a much cheaper price. Don't you dare waste ninety bucks on your attire, because you can use these websites for buying affordable yoga pants:

## 1. Boscovs

[Boscovs.com](http://www.Boscovs.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) has yoga pants for women and juniors. No matter what color you want on your waistband, you can find it on this site. Why buy from here? Because some of the prices are as low as seven dollars. It's hard to beat that.

## 2. Clothing under 10

The name of this site says it all. _Clothingunder10.com_ has yoga pants that are under ten dollars. If you want standard black, you can buy it here. If you want to look funky and wear neon, you can buy it here. Whatever you want, you can find it here with just one click. If you like yoga pants and love great savings, you might as well check it out.

## 3. American Eagle

You've probably bought a few things from [ae.com](http://www.ae.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) before. Since you already know you can trust this store, you shouldn't hesitate to buy yoga pants from their website. You're going to be spending a little more than ten bucks, but it'll be worth it, because you'll be wearing a brand-name that you can brag about.

## 4. Kohls

When all else fails, visit [kohls.com](http://www.kohls.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). They have everything that a woman could possibly want, which is why you shouldn't be surprised about their massive supply of yoga pants. It might take a little while to scroll through all of the options Kohls offers, but by the end of your search, your shopping cart will be filled with gorgeous goods.

## 5. Lane Bryant

Yoga pants aren't reserved for super skinny girls. They can look great on any body type, so if you need plus sized clothing, look no further. [Lanebryant.com](http://www.Lanebryant.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) has yoga pants for any size woman. They come in some pretty cute colors, so you don't have to worry about your clothes looking any less than fabulous.

## 6. Target

[Target.com](https://www.target.com/s/yoga+pants) is another store that always has your back. It's pretty popular, which means that you can probably save yourself from paying for shipping by just driving down to the nearest store. That way, you'll even get to try on what you want to buy before you buy it. Then you won't have to worry about the possibility of returning something you thought you'd love. It just makes things easier.

## 7. Express

This site is the most expensive of the stores on this list. However, in the grand scheme of things, [express.com](https://goo.gl/VOc9v2) isn't that pricey. Some people actually prefer to pay a bit more, because it makes them feel like their clothes will last longer and look better. So if you're happier spending a bit more money than necessary, this site will give you what you need without emptying your entire wallet.
