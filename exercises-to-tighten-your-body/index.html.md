# Exercises to Tighten Your Body for Girls Who Want to Get Rid of Jiggly Bits ...

If adding a little toned magic to your daily routine sounds appealing, you’re in the right place. Achieving a **toned body** that can confidently say "buh-bye" to those jiggly bits is not just about tough workouts, it's a lifestyle. Whether you’re a gym newbie or a seasoned fitness lover, effective **exercises to tighten your body** can bring spectacular results and have you rocking that dress or those jeans flawlessly.

The rise of social media #fitnessgoals has made achieving a **sculpted physique** more relatable and less intimidating. And hey, gimmicky gadgets can take a backseat. The focus is on good-old-fashioned sweat sessions, good nutrition, and a sprinkle of fun.

One thing I've learned during my fitness journey is that a **strength training routine** is paramount. We’ll delve into some amazing [core-strengthening](#1) moves that'll have you feeling like a fitness warrior in no time. Finding the right balance is key, not just in yoga poses but also in combining **cardio exercises** with strength training. Mixing things up with some energetic [aerobic sessions](#2) can be like throwing a dance party for your muscles.

Now, let’s sprinkle in a pinch of nutrition advice. Nutrition, my friends, is the unsung hero of fitness. A good [diet for muscle recovery](#3) ensures your muscles get the fuel they need to show off their hard-earned strength. Because let’s face it-after a kick-butt workout, they deserve some love, too.

Incorporating these practices will not only help you get rid of those pesky jiggly bits but also bring in a burst of confidence that’ll have you smiling every time you walk past a mirror. ## 1. Get Rid of Armpit Overhang

![shoulder, room, joint, structure, abdomen,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ig/tt/vkenwbgc59916f640089d283682336_600x600.jpg)

You know what I'm talking about. That little bit of jiggle that forms when you wear a strapless gown. Good news! By adding a few sets of push ups to your routine, you can help firm that area up. Start with a couple sets of 15 push ups and add more as you get stronger. You'll be amazed at how much firmer you are in the armpit area. Talk about one of the best exercises to tighten your body!

## 2. Get Rid of the Jiggle in Your Thighs

![undergarment, lingerie, shoulder, leg, thigh,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/is/xiiowfgi59916fd9a7e80843422249_600x600.jpg)

Whether you have jiggle on the outside or inside of your thighs, or even both areas, there is help. Most women tend to collect fat in the hip and thigh area, but you aren't doomed to being jiggly forever. Of course, cardio will burn that fat in no time, as long as you combine it with a healthy diet. Lunges are also a must in your tightening routine.

## 3. Banish Bra Bulge Once and for All

![shoulder, sitting, joint, physical fitness, standing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/l8/kt79dlr659916f3b09dc8331159490_600x749.jpg)

If you've noticed a bulging over your bra strap in the back, bent over rows are your new best friend. This move targets that area specifically and will help tighten and tone so that you can wear tank tops again without any embarrassment.

## 4. Tone down the Belly Pooch

![thigh, human leg, active undergarment, shoulder, abdomen,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mo/s1/u1afflh959917025631f9233352207_600x688.jpg)

The belly is one of the most common places for women to collect flab. Crunches are great, but you can't rely on them alone to tone things down. Add some planks to your workout routine. You're also going to have to do plenty of cardio to bust the fat and uncover the muscles beneath.

## 5. Lose the Underarm Flab

![undergarment, active undergarment, shoulder, physical fitness, thigh,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/us/po/l5ob5w5559917032c45ec184738650_600x600.jpg)

This is a problem spot for many women, but it's time to quit covering up with sleeves. Start doing a few sets of chair dips every couple of days and that area will tone right up with ease. It's time to stock up on tank tops now.

Where do you have jiggle? Which of these moves is going to be your new go to?
