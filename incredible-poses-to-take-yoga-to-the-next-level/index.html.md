# 21 Incredible Yoga Poses to Take You to the Next Level ...

It takes concentration and focus to achieve perfect yoga poses – even some that are considered suitable for beginners. And then some yogis manage to twist and bend into the most incredible shapes. Want to see what you could achieve after much practice and desiccation? Are you ready to turn yourself into a human pretzel?

## 1. Lotus Handstand

![Lotus Handstand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ow/my/uy25veyy55e45cdb31c1f671897096_600x600.jpg)

If you can't get into the lotus position without using your hands yet, you'll not be able to achieve this pose.

## 2. Flying Crow

![Flying Crow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/mo/mj7ptqam55e45e1b25637468239420_600x431.jpg)

It takes great flexibility and incredible strength to be able to bring your foot to rest on your tricep in this position.

## 3. Dropback

![Dropback](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yy/gu/f9rildem55e45cdb7b321473343903_600x771.jpg)

If you can achieve a full wheel, the next challenge is to drop into it from a standing position.

## 4. King Pigeon Pose

![King Pigeon Pose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q8/l5/swqgu6ny55e45cdb40b86597510166_600x600.jpg)

This will put your "bendyness" to the test.

## 5. Side Plank Pose

![Side Plank Pose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ss/wq/xha7vi7355e45e9b86a28642556528_600x584.jpg)

The plank is one of the staples of any fitness routine and here is how to make it super challenging.

## 6. Iron Cross Headstand

![Iron Cross Headstand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ae/t2/iw4su91i55e460078cd66337308633_600x600.jpg)

The iron cross headstand is an inversion that needs a strong focus.

## 7. Flying Pigeon

![Flying Pigeon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cx/xx/q3kkgc8a55e45fb257545567085774_600x435.jpg)

This pose strengthens the abdominal muscles and arms. You'll really feel it stretch those hamstrings.

## 8. Firefly B

![Firefly B](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hw/xu/wt5h57rd55e45cdd21333841383260_600x461.jpg)

In this variation of the arm balance firefly, you fold so far in half that your head ends up behind your legs.

## 9. Hummingbird

![Hummingbird](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/cz/tt2cuod755e45cdd446d7205321069_600x400.jpg)

Use your upper body strength to balance on your arms and open up your hips.

## 10. Peacock

![Peacock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ei/uv/ycuxsjbl55e45cdce3f9b130045289_600x400.jpg)

To achieve the position of a posing peacock, you'll have to concentrate your weight in your wrists.

## 11. Peacock Variation

![Peacock Variation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/m9/doomr0fc55e45cdd81b0f322675916_550x418.jpg)

As if the peacock isn't difficult enough – this variation is super scary. Could you get your legs into that position without using your hands?

## 12. One-Legged Staff

![One-Legged Staff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/hf/knifrc0355e4608774f32595874282_400x494.jpg)

A killer combination of headstand and back bend.

## 13. Handstand Scorpion

![Handstand Scorpion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ag/xp/b8k20lmr55e45cddf1623086976308_600x600.jpg)

Adding an intense back bend really tests your prowess at the handstand.

## 14. Locust Scorpion

![Locust Scorpion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qw/pm/cv0iw30t55e45cdf97b31031837462_593x618.jpg)

This pose will test your spinal flexibility to the max.

## 15. Lifted Thunderbolt

![Lifted Thunderbolt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p5/en/q6h03ape55e45cdeba5b6979469921_600x600.jpg)

You'll need to reach for the sky to get the most from this stretch – that is, if you can actually get in this pose in the first place.

## 16. Dancer Split

![Dancer Split](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ad/gq/ck91kkwy55e45cdecded3759166939_550x550.jpg)

This adds extra stretch to the usual dancer pose – especially on the hamstrings. From the standard dancer pose, straighten your lifted leg into a split position.

## 17. Frog

![Frog](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/gt/dnntomeb55e45cdf01ac9563031828_550x550.jpg)

Your quads will want to croak when you put them through this pose.

## 18. Lotus Headstand

![Lotus Headstand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b1/pb/fsefs3wd55e45cdf93a12439834861_600x471.jpg)

Another pose where you need to be able to get into the lotus position without using your hands.

## 19. Big Toe Bow

![Big Toe Bow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ux/ou/b2d05bq155e45ce06a121371212839_550x550.jpg)

Take the bow pose to the next level by holding your feet in the arched back position. This will seriously flex your spine.

## 20. Pinching Shoulders Headstand

![Pinching Shoulders Headstand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zw/fl/je236p5d55e45ce0bcd60386602240_600x600.jpg)

Are you ready to balance on your elbows and head?

## 21. Eight Crooked Limbs

![Eight Crooked Limbs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/x7/b7zxjm7155e45ce089c87170075793_600x400.jpg)

There's an extreme twist to this arm balance pose.
