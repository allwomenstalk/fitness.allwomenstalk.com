# 7 Fitness Video Games to Try for a Fun Workout ...

If you're bored with your current workout routine, fitness video games are a great way to add some variety. After all, shouldn't exercising be fun? With fitness video games like these, you can have a blast, while burning some calories.

## 1. Zumba Fitness World Party - Nintendo Wii U

![Zumba Fitness World Party - Nintendo Wii U](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/yx/puz8yfen_357x500.jpg)

Zumba Fitness World Party is one of the hottest fitness video games around. It's packed with tons of Zumba routines that are great for both beginners and those looking for a more advanced workout. This game also allows you to set your own personal goals and track your calories burned with each workout. If you've never tried Zumba, there's no need to feel intimidated. This game is designed so you can learn the moves at your own pace.

## 2. Gold Gym Dance Workout - Nintendo Wii

![Gold Gym Dance Workout - Nintendo Wii](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/gp/httmx2gz_392x550.jpg)

If you're looking for a workout that you can enjoy doing with a friend, this one is a great choice. Gold Gym Dance Workout is a great way to get fit while having fun doing dances like the salsa, rumba, and samba. If you don't like any the preloaded routines or get bored with them, you can easily create your own circuit workout.

## 3. The Biggest Loser Ultimate Workout - Xbox 360

![The Biggest Loser Ultimate Workout - Xbox 360](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ff/u2/sprk00de_355x500.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Are you a fan of The Biggest Loser? If so, you've got to try this workout. This video game features over 100 different moves that are easy for beginners to do. It also has 50 healthy recipes that are easy to make. If you're trying to lose weight, you can also use this game to keep track of your calorie intake.

## 4. My Fitness Coach - NIntendo Wii

![My Fitness Coach - NIntendo Wii](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dj/h3/jor0s2yd_356x497.jpg)

If you tend to get tired of doing the same workout routine, My Fitness Coach is for you. This exercise game features over 500 workouts to ensure that you never get bored. You can also choose from a variety of different types of music to listen to while you workout like salsa and 80's. You can even chart your progress to see how much your fitness level has improved over time.

## 5. Your Shape Fitness Evolved - 2012 - Xbox 360

![Your Shape Fitness Evolved - 2012 - Xbox 360](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ee/gf/j2qbvdd9_355x500.jpg)

With over 90 hours of activities, this fitness game has something for everyone. It features boot camp workouts, dance routines, and cardio boxing. It's also super easy to customize and will allow you to create a workout that will match your current fitness level. Your Shape Fitness Evolved also allows you compete with friends and share your results and workouts with them.

## 6. Gold Gym Cardio Workout - Nintendo Wii

![Gold Gym Cardio Workout - Nintendo Wii](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/en/ap/tykznpt7_356x500.jpg)

This video game has everything you need to get in shape. It features tons of great workouts like boxing and running. Plus, this game will make you feel like you're really at the gym. You can also easily track your weight loss and calories burned. It will even automatically adjust your workout to match your current fitness level.

## 7. Wii Fit plus - Nintendo Wii

![Wii Fit plus - Nintendo Wii](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lg/c1/ssatqt7y_385x385.jpg)

Wii Fit Plus is a cool game that will allow you to customize your workout routine regardless of how much time you have. This is a great way to get a complete workout in when you are strapped for time. Wii Fit Plus also has a variety of workouts to choose from including yoga and aerobics. It even keeps track of how often you do each routine.

Fitness video games are a great and inexpensive way to add some variety to your workout. Do you enjoy them? What is your favorite?
