## 1. Traditional Kegels

You can do Kegels in class, on a date, or during a job interview. It doesn't matter where you are or who you're with, because no one will be able to tell that you're completing the exercise. All you have to do is find the muscles that you use to stop urinating. Now you should squeeze them for a few seconds and release them for a few seconds. ## 2. Pelvic Tilt

Rest on your back with your knees bent. Now rotate your pelvis and then lower yourself back down. That's all it takes to do a pelvic tilt. Not only will it strengthen your PC muscles, but it will also strengthen your glutes and your lower back. The benefits are endless, so you might as well give this exercise a try.

## 3. Squats

You've probably done squats a few times before. You're basically going to be standing up straight, with your feet shoulder-width apart, and then bending your knees like you're about to sit in a chair. In addition to strengthening your PC muscles, the exercise will enhance your core, improve your flexibility, and strengthen your lower body.

## 4. The Butterfly

This exercise requires you to sit on the floor with your feet together and your shoulders back. Drive your knees down into the floor and back up again. It will stretch your body, giving you stronger PC muscles and sexier legs.

## 5. Benwa Balls

You can use them for sexual pleasure, but you can also use them to strengthen your PC muscles. You don't actually have to do anything once they're inside of you.
