# 5 Greatest YouTube Workout Channels to See Now ...

What are the **best YouTube workout channels**? Remember back in the day when if you wanted to find some inspiration for your fitness journey you would either have to sign up for classes at the gym or head out to the store to buy one of the many celebrity exercise DVDs that filled up the shelves? Thank goodness we don’t have to go through that anymore! All you need these days to find a workout regime that suits and motivates you is to an internet connection. YouTube has spawned many trends over the years, and the one we are looking at today is the corner of the video site that is dedicated to fitness gurus. Here are five of the **best YouTube workout channels**.

## 1. Blogilates

This is channel created by Cassey Ho, a girl who knows everything about working out! Her videos are usually around the ten-minute mark and all focus on different muscle groups. If you follow Blogilates, you can be sure that you never miss out an area of your body in your fitness journey. This is definitely one of the **best YouTube workout channels** out there.

[youtube.com](https://www.youtube.com/user/blogilates)

## 2. Fitness Blender

This is a channel hosted by Daniel and Kelli, a husband and wife fitness duo that put lots of fun into living a healthy lifestyle. They have already uploaded more than 500 online workouts for you to go through, ranging from beginner to expert level, and they cover a range of styles from yoga to HIIT. You’re bound to find something that you love!

[youtube.com](https://www.youtube.com/user/FitnessBlender)

## 3. Yoga with Adriene

If you are looking for a channel that presents yoga in a beginner style fashion, then Adriene is your girl. Her vibe is super casual and her instruction videos feel really welcoming and inclusive. Her warm attitude is really infectious and is the perfect kind of attitude to have when you are looking to get into relaxing and carefree yoga.

[youtube.com](https://www.youtube.com/user/yogawithadriene)

## 4. Yoga with Tim

If you’re looking for something a little more challenging in the yoga world, then definitely try out Tim’s channel. Longer videos, less basic instruction, but still the same level of energy and insight that makes Tim’s workouts really fun and beneficial.

[youtube.com](https://www.youtube.com/user/yogawithtim)

## 5. Dance Fitness with Jessica

If you are intimidated by exercise, then do it via something familiar like dancing! Dance Fitness with Jessica is the perfect channel for you because she incorporates her moves into modern pop songs that provide you with so much fun and joy you will forget that you are even exercising!

[youtube.com](https://www.youtube.com/user/FitnesswithJessica)
