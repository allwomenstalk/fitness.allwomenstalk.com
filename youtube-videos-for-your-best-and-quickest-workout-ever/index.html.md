# YouTube Videos for Your Best and Quickest Workout Ever ...

If you’re someone who loves working out, but in the privacy of your own home, it can be challenging to find different workouts you can do that are just as great as a workout you’d get in the gym. One of the biggest benefits of working out at home versus in the gym is that it’s easier to get a quick workout in. What would cut an hour or two out of your day at the gym can easily be done in 15 minutes at home. If you’re looking for the best and quickest workouts you can do from home, these routines are sure to get your heart pumping and your body moving!

## 1. The Ultimate Butt, Back, and Arms Challenge

If you’re a Pilates girl, this move is the perfect Pilates move for you! It’s quick and easy, even if you’re a Pilates beginner, and can easily be added into whatever Pilates workout you’re doing. Plus, it helps that it targets so many different areas on your body with just one simple move!

## 2. 10 Minute P90X3 Workout

I don’t know about you, but I’ve always been curious about P90X. However, I’ve just never wanted to spend the money on a class or video if I wasn’t sure that I was going to absolutely love it. If you’re in the same predicament, try this workout sometime soon! It gives you a full body workout in just 10 minutes, so not only is it great if you want to try P90X, but it’s also great if you don’t have a lot of time on your hands!

## 3. 5-Minute Crop Top Workout

Summer is creeping up on us sooner rather than later, and I think we would all like to get in on the crop top trend that’s sweeping the style world. If you’re like me and want to wear a crop top but don’t love your abs yet, try doing this 5-minute crop top workout every day for a month. I promise you that you’ll notice a difference immediately!

## 4. 7-Minute Workout to Lose Weight Fast, Burn Fat, and Tone Body

Some people are more into a classic workout without all of the frills that come along with a lot of workouts today. If that’s you, and you want a simple workout of jumping jacks, wall sits, push-ups, and more that will get the job done, try this quick 7-minute workout on for size!

## 5. The Most Effective Squat Challenge

I seriously did this squat challenge every day for a month, and it was life changing. Squat challenges are not a new concept. You’ll find them all over the Internet. However, I didn’t like how small you start out in most squat challenges. I liked that this squat challenge was 100 squats no matter what day it was. If you’re looking for a squat challenge that will transform your body, this is the one!

## 6. 10-Minute Cardio Abs Dance Workout

When it comes to cardio, I love a good dance workout. Having been a dancer for 15 years, I love when working out doesn’t feel like a chore. If you’re looking for a similar workout, try this quick and easy 10-minute dance workout. It’s a fun way to blast through your daily cardio!

## 7. 15-Minute Cardio and Total Body Toning Boot Camp Workout

If you’re looking for something longer, but still not too long, try this total body toning workout. I think that this workout is especially great if you’ve always wanted to try a boot camp at your local gym but have been pretty nervous about it. This may just give you a peek into what to expect!
