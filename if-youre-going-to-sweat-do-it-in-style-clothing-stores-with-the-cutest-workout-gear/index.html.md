# If You're Going to Sweat, do It in Style\- Clothing Stores with the Cutest Workout Gear ...

Where are all of my girls who love working out? I’ve recently discovered just how much I love working out after nearly 20 years of thinking that I hated it, which means that I’m now on the hunt for cute workout clothes. Are you on a similar hunt? Well, join me, because I’ve done my research, and I’ve found the best places to buy workout clothes, especially if you’re on a budget and still want to look cute. These stores have some of the best workout clothes, even if you’ve never thought of shopping at them for your workout clothes. (Charlotte Russe, anyone? That one surprised me!) The next time you go on the hunt for workout clothes, you may be surprised what you can find in your own mall!

## 1. Charlotte Russe

I recently perused Charlotte Russe for the first time in a long time and was so shocked by just how great the workout gear selection was. In fact, I don’t think that there was even a workout gear section the last time I went. I love the workout selection at Charlotte Russe because not only are the clothes good for working out in, but they’re also super cute!

## 2. Forever 21

If you’ve ever stepped inside of a Forever 21 or even just looked around the website, you probably know just how great Forever 21’s workout gear section is. If your Forever 21 is one of the bigger stores, than there’s probably an entire, overwhelmingly large section dedicated to just workout gear that you can choose from!

## 3. Target

One of my favorite things about Target is that fairly often, if you love a designer brand but hate the price tag, you can find “dupes” at Target. That’s especially true in the workout section, where a lot of the workout gear reminds me of Lulu Lemon with Target price tags! You don’t have to spend a lot of money to look cute!

## 4. Old Navy

Old Navy is known for its cheap basics that are cute and stylish-something that is definitely easier said than done. That description is especially true in the workout gear section. In fact, Old Navy has recently launched a line called “Old Navy Active” with such a wide variety. Everything looks like it’d cost a small fortune, but barely any items cost more than $30.

## 5. H&M

If you don’t like typical workout gear, try checking out H&M. H&M offers a ton of different patterns and colors that you wouldn’t find on workout gear anywhere else. This line is great especially if you don’t love bright colors, because most workout clothes come in bright colors. This line offers a lot of muted colors and patterns!

## 6. Amazon

Amazon sells almost everything under the sun. However, when was the last time you were on the hunt for workout gear and looked to Amazon? It’s not exactly the first place that comes to mind, is it? Amazon has anything and everything you could need when it comes to workout gear, no matter the function or style.

## 7. Lulu Lemon

Lulu Lemon is the Alpha and Omega when it comes to workout gear, but I don’t think I even need to tell many people that. If you’re willing to spend the higher price tag for clothes as cute as Lulu Lemon, this is definitely the store for you!

Where do you shop for workout clothes? Give me some new ideas in the comments!
