# Work out While Watching These Motivational YouTube Channels ...

I’m going to be honest right now. I’ve never enjoyed working out. It has just never really been my thing. I will exercise, but to me, it’s worse than pulling teeth. I hate it. I hate going to the gym and feeling judged, and I like to work out at my own pace. When I discovered fitness YouTube videos, my life changed forever. I could pick and choose the workouts that I wanted to do, I could do them at my own pace, I didn’t have to pay to watch them, and I loved what I was doing. These days, working out isn’t a chore for me. I look forward to it, and it’s thanks to these videos!

## 1. Blogilates

Not only does Cassey offer a bunch of different fitness videos, but she also posts fitness challenges, recipes, and a ton of other inspirational tips to help get you through your toughest days. If you’re just starting out and looking for someone peppy and fun to help get you moving, Cassey is your girl!

## 2. Club Fitz

Let me be honest here. I have tried every workout under the sun, but I’ve never been able to stick it out with any workout for more than a few weeks. After finding Lauren and Kelsi’s videos, I look forward to working out because it doesn’t feel like a workout. Since finding their videos, I haven’t even taken a day off because I love doing their choreography that much.

## 3. BeFiT

This YouTube channel features some of the biggest trainers teaching a variety of different classes. Each video is at least 10 minutes long, which means that you can get a quick workout or something longer if you have the time. It’s very customizable to what you want to do and what you want to target!

## 4. The Daily Hiit

Do you love high intensity interval training but don’t have the time or money to commit to a gym? This website is so much more than a YouTube channel, it’s a movement. While their channel is technically called BodyRock, the Daily Hiit is also a website that offers inspiration and even a workout reminder email to motivate you to get your workout in! It will be revolutionary in your life.

## 5. Barre3

Founded by Christy Turlington Burns, this yoga and pilates hybrid is a sure way to start your morning off the right way. If you’re looking for a way to work on your mental and physical health at the same time, Barre 3 may just be your perfect workout!

## 6. Love Zumba

For all of the Zumba lovers out there, Zumba can now come to your home for free! If you can’t find your favorite Zumba class in your area, you’ll never have to worry anymore because Zumba is now on YouTube! There are music videos, workout routines, and even trailers for different conventions you may want to attend if they come to a city near you!

## 7. Dance Fitness with Jessica

Few “dance fitness” instructors provide as much variety as Jessica does. If you’re not a fan of hip-hop music but love country, you’ll be surprised by the variety you have when it comes to picking songs you like. I always feel like it’s much easier to get a workout in when you don’t hate the music you’re working out to!

What are your favorite fitness YouTube channels? Give me some suggestions in the comments!
