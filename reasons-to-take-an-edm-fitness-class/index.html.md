# All the Reasons to Take an EDM Fitness Class to Change up Your Fitness Routine ...

Curious about **reasons to take an EDM fitness class**? Keep reading...

You've reached a difficult part in your workout and you feel like you've hit a wall physically. What are you going to do to motivate yourself to continue working out? This is just one of the **reasons to take an EDM fitness class**. Adrenaline pumping EDM music will bring your workout to the next level.

## 1. Take an EDM Fitness Class

![undergarment, active undergarment, physical fitness, thigh, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/rv/se6r8sbo59b049ba19779324049512_600x750.jpg)

One of the best **reasons to take an EDM fitness class** is that you can workout with others in a party style environment! Who doesn't love a good party? It's a great opportunity to meet new people, and listen to motivating tunes while working out. You can find classes at a local gym, through planned meet ups, and various events.

## 2. Anyone Can Participate in the EDM Fitness Classes

![clothing, active undergarment, undergarment, shoulder, abdomen,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/qu/h2fkgkfn59b04a1ed7e97954806862_600x600.jpg)

Weights are not needed in an EDM fitness class, all ages are welcome, and the trainer leads the class using basic dance moves and exercises for different skill levels.

## 3. Choosing the Songs on Your EDM Playlist

![human hair color, forehead, fashion accessory, hair accessory, audio,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/av/vp/pyiizux859b04b7b8fe01707773188_600x600.jpg)

If you would prefer to work out solo, EDM is great for this as well. Billboard.com recommends dance tracks including, “progressive, electro, tech, deep, and future house tracks.” It is also possible to look for vocal-heavy tracks, and harder beats. You can listen to EDM during a warmup, or for an hour long work out.

## 4. Where to Find EDM Playlists

![room, active undergarment, shoulder, thigh, structure,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/ev/v7hbueza59b04db4b634a771203476_600x750.jpg)

An EDM playlist put together by Billboard.com includes, Alesso, deadmau#5, and Tiesto. Spotify has a playlist called, The Best of EDM 2017, which is updated daily. And, also check out EDM YouTube channels, or EDM mix CDs.
