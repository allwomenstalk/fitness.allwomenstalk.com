# How to Use Yoga to Make Yourself Feel Beautiful ...

A great yoga session is one of the best things in life, am I right? After all, you leave feeling healthy and energized at the same time that you relieve stress and feel some relaxation too. Who could want more? It turns out that yoga is good for a lot of things, one of them being that it can make you feel better about how you look. Sign me up! Here are several reasons why a yoga session will leave you feeling your most beautiful self. So don’t forget to make it to your studio today!

## 1. It Builds Muscle

A lean and muscular body is both womanly and attractive, which makes your yoga session especially ideal. Holding all those poses works the muscles all over your body, which is a great way to look toned and beautiful all over. When your muscles are sculpted, you look like you are super healthy because you are, which is absolutely gorgeous in my book.

## 2. Enhances Body Functions

There is a large body of research that says that yoga can improve the function of many of your body’s systems. When your body is working optimally and everything is humming along as it should, you look healthy and beautiful. I can’t say for sure one way or the other, but educating yourself on the studies and talking to your doctor is a great way to see if yoga can help you with a health condition that you feel interferes with your looks.

## 3. Body Awareness

Maybe you’ve never considered how your spine plays a role in your appearance. Or how your feet or neck do. What yoga can do for you is help you gain a good awareness of your body and how it works and what you can do with it. And there’s nothing more beautiful than that!

## 4. Improves Self-Esteem

By going to your yoga studio regularly, you get stronger and it really helps to relieve stress. That’s great for you because it helps with your self-esteem. When you feel good about yourself and how you look, it translates to feeling more beautiful on a day to day basis. Who knew exercise could do something like that for you?

## 5. Improves Circulation

Consistent yoga sessions improve your blood flow, which leaves you with glowing, radiant and healthy skin. That translates to a healthy look that most of the world equates with beauty. The deep breathing you participate in during yoga helps get blood to your heart so circulation is more efficient throughout your body. That’s something you can’t beat, so get to the studio today!

## 6. Boosts Healthy Choices

Have you ever noticed how one healthy choice naturally leads to others? You don’t want to undo all your hard work at yoga with a huge soda on the way home, right? By engaging in exercise, yoga included, you spur yourself to make healthy choices in other areas of your life, including making healthy eating choices. When you do this, your body looks healthier and that’s beautiful.

## 7. Aids in Weight Loss

You probably can’t rely on yoga alone to lose unwanted weight, but it does burn some calories, which can come in handy if you’re trying to shed some pounds. Don’t get me wrong, beauty comes in all sizes, but if you don’t feel beautiful at your current size, adding yoga to your routine can help. Combined with healthy eating and plenty of cardio, you’ll be feeling as beautiful as you are in no time.

How often do you go to yoga? Does it make you feel beautiful?
