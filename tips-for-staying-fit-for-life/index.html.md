# Pro Tips on Staying Fit for Life ...

Are you looking for some **tips for staying fit for life**? Staying fit is a question of attitude and healthy people view it as a lifestyle. Beyond the physical signs of aging, you may notice other changes like decreased energy, altered sleep pattern, and the onset of health problems. Well, the good news is taking charge of your well-being can help you thrive. Here are some great **tips for staying fit for life**.

## 1. Follow the Canada Food Guide

This eating plan assists individuals with making healthy food choices for their age. It offers valuable tools for a balanced diet and is one of the best **tips for staying fit for life**.

## 2. Maintain a Healthy Weight

You can make this happen by reducing the time you spend being inactive, paying attention to what, when, and how much you eat, and maintaining a body mass index (BMI) between 19 and 25.

## 3. Use Your Brain

Do activities that help keep your mind sharp as you age. Some examples of what to do include increasing your level of social interaction, learning new skills, playing challenging games, and doing other activities that require an engaged mind.

## 4. Exercise Daily

Engage in a regimen that lasts for at least 30 minutes. Walking is a low impact and easy way to get moving and stay in shape.

## 5. Drink in Moderation

Drink alcoholic beverages in moderation. Drinking two glasses of wine a day could actually be good for your brain and may even help stave off Alzheimer's disease, but over time, excessive alcohol use can lead to the development of chronic diseases and other serious health problems.
