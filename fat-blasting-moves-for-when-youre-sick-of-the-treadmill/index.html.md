# 9 Fat Blasting Moves for when You're Sick of the Treadmill ...

Sure, running on the treadmill is a fantastic workout. It burns loads of calories and tones the muscles in your lower body. But just because it's a great workout doesn't mean it's the only one that works. If you're sick and tired of your time on the treadmill, there are loads of other ways to blast fat and get in shape. Try any of these, courtesy of [womanista.com](https://womanista.com/wellness/2017/02/24/9-fat-blasting-moves-that-ll-blow-your-treadmill-workout-away), and you'll be loving your workout in a whole new way.

## 1. [Ball Slams](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/09/21/ball-slams/)

![human action, overhead press, person, barbell, fitness professional,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ze/pz/gc3at8k458ef85b014287729241262_600x397.jpg)

Start in a squat stance with toes slightly turned out. Reach and grab the ball while keeping your chest lifted. Push out of the squat while lifting the ball overhead and dropping it behind your head like a triceps extension. Slam the ball into the ground while simultaneously dropping into your squat; catch the ball as it bounces up and repeat.

## 2. [Leap Frog](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/09/23/leap-frog/)

![human action, dance, sports, arm, modern dance,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/de/dx/sbipvakh58ef86e328239910938803_600x600.jpg)

Begin in a squatting position with your feet outside your shoulders and your hips dropped back and down. Reach down toward the floor with your hands while keeping your chest lifted. Now explode out of your squat by pushing through your legs and throwing your hips forward. Land with soft knees or in a deep squat; repeat.

## 3. [Plank Jack and Jump](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/10/16/plank-jack-and-jump/)

![human action, sports, physical fitness, yoga, arm,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/wh/aly92hm858ef86e3a5e94907539705_600x185.jpg)

Start in a high plank with your shoulders over your wrists with your hips in line with your body. Jack the feet out to the edge of your mat without moving the upper body or hips. Jack the feet back together, then jump feet to the outside of your hands while your hands remain planted on the floor; repeat.

## 4. [Power Knee Grab](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/09/29/power-knee-grab/)

![human action, person, performing arts, dance, sports,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/y6/ftznpgev58ef86e39c3eb447206928_600x600.jpg)

Start with your feet hip-width apart and extend one behind you as the front leg bends. Simultaneously extend your arms out and above your head. Quickly pull the back leg through to the front and bring your hands down, tucking elbows into your sides powerfully as you feel your abs contract; repeat.

## 5. [Sweeping Skaters](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/07/27/sweeping-skaters/)

![human action, performing arts, sports, entertainment, modern dance,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y8/w2/ionzqpw258ef86e3b3e69398460396_600x397.jpg)

From standing, cross one leg in front of you and drop into a lunge. Reach your opposite hand all the way down to the floor while letting your chest drop. Next jump up, swinging your arms overhead and making a big circle with your arms; you should get both feet off the floor at the same time. Switch your feet, leaping from side to side. Cross the opposite leg in front of your body, lunge and touch the floor. Repeat.

## 6. [Plie Squat Jump](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/07/09/pli-squat-jump/)

![human action, pink, magenta, sports, arm,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/po/m2/ov7yos0v58ef86e459583777478313_600x542.jpg)

Step your feet out wider than your shoulders and turn them out 45 degrees. The hips will rise up and down, not back and down like a normal squat; keep your chest lifted. Power out of the bottom of your squat and jump; make sure your knees are bent when you hit the ground again. Repeat.

## 7. [BOSU Side To Side Taps](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/06/22/bosu-side-to-side-taps/)

![human action, person, strength training, arm, muscle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/in/m4/ue2w4jva58ef86e423dfc499376910_600x442.jpg)

Place one foot in the center of the BOSU ball and bend the knee, putting your weight onto this foot. Let the other foot step out to the side and keep your chest lifted. Press of the BOSU and switch feet; the switch happens quickly while moving side to side every second or faster.

## 8. [Jump Shot](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/10/30/jump-shot/)

![human action, performing arts, dance, sports, entertainment,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p1/j3/fjbldpcj58ef86e5b3a6a614085905_600x600.jpg)

Turn your body at a 45 degree angle with your feet close together and knees bent. Get your hands up like you’re holding a basketball; keep the elbows up and look up, too. Jump up straight and raise the arms like you’re shooting a ball overheard; land with soft knees and step and turn to face the other side to repeat.

## 9. [The Power](http://womanista.com/wellness/2015/06/22/the-power/)

![human action, arm, sport venue, sports, physical fitness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/an/cpjo53hy58ef86e603736535473456_600x397.jpg)

Place one foot in the center of the BOSU and drop into a lunge. Make sure both knees are bent at a 90 degree angle and the chest is lifted. Bring the back foot to the side in a wide stance, then drop into a squat by letting the hips fall back and down; keep the chest up. Push of the outside foot and pull the knee up to hip height. At the same time, jump up and raise your arm overhead to power up the jump. Land with soft knees, then step back into your lunge to being the next rep.
