# 7 Super\-Important Tips for Girls Going to the Gym for the First Timeℹ️ ...

Even though getting off the couch and going to the gym can be a pretty intimidating process, starting your fitness journey with a bit of education can really help you accomplish all your fitness goals. If you want to be successful, then you need to start off on the right track. If you start on the wrong foot, you may develop bad habits and you may sabotage your fitness goals. If it’s been a while since you last hit the gym, you should know that there are a few rules you should follow since they will help you make excellent progress right away and you’ll avoid feeling lost or out of place at the start of your fitness journey. Here are 7 important tips for gym newbies that you should consider:

## 1. Push beyond the Intimidation and Go for It

Going to the gym for the first time can be a bit intimidating since you might worry that you won’t know how to use certain machines or how to do certain exercises. But, the truth is that things are not as hard as they seem, you can always go and ask a trainer to give you a 2-minute tutorial on how to properly use the machine in question. After a few weeks, you will feel more inspired than intimidated by the people who are a bit more athletic than you.

## 2. Make Fitness a Habit

If you want to see some results, then you need to make fitness a habit, just like brushing your teeth every morning. Even if it may seem like an impossible task, especially when you are really busy and you don’t even have enough time to sleep, it’s essential to work out at least 3 or 4 times a week if you want to achieve your fitness goals.

## 3. Meet with a Trainer

You should always take advantage of your free sessions with a trainer that are included in your gym membership. Don’t be afraid to ask questions! They will show you how to use some of the equipment and how to do the exercises correctly.

## 4. Dress Appropriately

Keep in mind that the gym is not a fashion show, so if you worry too much about your fancy attire, this can take energy away from your workout. Just keep it simple because inappropriate or loose clothing can get caught on machines. Also, avoid excessive makeup or perfume and make sure that your hair is secured.

## 5. Learn Form Your Mistakes

If you are a beginner, just keep in mind that nobody started out as an expert. They all made mistakes and so will you. Just pay attention to those mistakes and learn from them. This way, you will be able to tailor your workout program to your own needs and this means better results in the future.

## 6. Familiarize Yourself with Good Gym Etiquette

When you go to the gym, you should always practice good manners and always be respectful of your fellow gym goers. If you want to feel more confident, you should know all the unwritten rules and the gym won’t seem like such an unfriendly place anymore.

## 7. Do What You Enjoy

If you are a gym newbie, then you should start by doing easy workouts, things that you enjoy, like a few minutes of moderate activity on the rowing machine or some easy lifting. As you go to the gym more often, you will push yourself more and you’ll refine your workouts. Just do things that you enjoy because anything is better than sitting on the couch all day.

Going to the gym for the first time can be a bit intimidating but with a bit of patience and some effort, you will manage to overcome that obstacle and you will start working toward reaching your fitness goals.
