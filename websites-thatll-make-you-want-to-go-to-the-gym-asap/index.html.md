# 7 Websites Thatll Make You Want to Go to the Gym ASAP ...

There are days when you need a little extra motivation to go to the gym and do your workout routine. The good news is that there a quite a few amazing websites that will help you find the energy and the focus you need to exercise. These websites are filled with motivational tools and inspirational stories, they will offer you insight, different points of view and different features that will help you reach your workout goals. Here are 7 websites that will make you want to go to the gym ASAP:

## 1. Sparkpeople.com

This is the largest online weight-loss community, with more than 8 million members worldwide encouraging each other to reach their goals and pursue all their dreams. If you sign up, they will even offer you a personalized plan that will help you reach your weight loss goals. This website is also filled with lots of motivational articles, mobile apps and trackers and you will even be able to find personalized fitness plans, recipes, videos, calorie counters and even meal plans.

## 2. Stickk.com

This is one amazing site that will definitely motivate you to reach your goals. All you have to do is select your fitness, productivity, health or nutrition goal, plug in your credit card number and pledge a specific amount of money to a charity or to a political party that you don’t support or don’t like. If you don’t reach your goal, your money will go to that organization that you are not too fond of.

## 3. Beforeyouwerehot.com

I love their slogan: “Because every swan was once an ugly duckling.” This website contains a collection of users’ not-so-hot pictures from when they were younger plus photos of what they look like now. So, if you ever doubt that you can change or improve certain things about yourself, go visit this website and see how far these people came.

## 4. Shape.com

If you ever need a little bit of motivation to reach your weight loss and fitness goals, just go and take a look at Shape Magazine’s website. It’s filled with wonderful articles on weight loss, fitness, healthy eating and healthy lifestyle. You can find tons of great advice there on how to pursue your dreams and how to create a healthy and happy lifestyle both for yourself and for your family.

## 5. Healthywage.com

This is another great website that will help you find the motivation you need to reach all your fitness goals. The beautiful thing about them is that they actually pay you to lose weight and stay healthy. Just make a weight loss bet, find a prize you like and make your wager, stay on track throughout the entire contest, meet your goal and win your prize.

## 6. WeightLossBuddy.com

This is another free online community that will help you reach your fitness goals and maintain a healthy lifestyle. It’s very similar to a social networking site since it allows you to create a profile page, make new buddies and share photos and motivation with them. You can also find a lot of great resources like weight loss tools (calorie counters, BMI calculator) and tips and tricks on how to stay healthy and fit.

## 7. Weightview.com

This site allows you to view how you would look after losing a few pounds, more specifically, at your target weight. You just have to upload a photo of yourself, specify how much weight you would like to lose and they will send you back an image of yourself at your target weight. You can print that photo and stick it on your fridge, on your mirror or somewhere you can see it often and it will motivate you to work hard to reach all your goals.

There are a lot of websites filled with great advice on how to lose weight and how to stay motivated to reach all your fitness goals. I just mentioned a few in this little article, but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. What other websites can make you go to the gym ASAP? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
