# These Cardios Are Amazing for Weight Loss ...

I confess: I used to loathe cardio. But a few years ago, I discovered it doesn't have to be a chore, and in fact, it can even be - gasp! - fun. So whether you're looking for a new cardio exercise to help you lose weight, stay fit, or just have more energy, here are the best cardios for weight loss, fitness, and energy (see what I did there)?

## 1. Trail Running

![Trail Running](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/su/sf8yke4c5540eb85cf0d1544582217_500x500.jpg)

Source: [Bff's](https://weheartit.com/entry/113380692/search?context_type=search&amp;context_user=_littleeprincess&amp;page=5&amp;query=trail+running)

So it doesn't look like much - running on a trail - but even without much of an elevation change, trail running is so much more of a cardio workout than even regular running. OMG!

![Elliptical Machine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n8/ri/or6b7h3f5540eb86e222c273104732_600x600.jpg)

Source: [Burn More Calories on the](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/529173024936205275/)

## 4. Bicycling

![Bicycling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/pg/kjz6yuuc5540eb8644019966794435_500x500.jpg)

If the new Soul Cycle craze is a bit intense for you, no worries - just hop on your bicycle and ride. It's still an excellent cardio workout!

## 5. Stand-up Paddleboard

![Stand-up Paddleboard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ra/s4/arfevh1r5540eb86628a1438339540_500x500.jpg)

## 6. Stairs (or Stair-Climber)

![Stairs (or Stair-Climber)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b3/to/gp816njn5540eb87f095c988589545_500x500.jpg)

Source: [Run fast??](https://weheartit.com/entry/163856995/search?context_type=search&amp;context_user=FeliciaKlingwall&amp;query=stairs+running)

Your legs will be SO SORE the next day, but using the Stair Climber at the gym, or better yet, climbing actual stairs, is sure to get your heart thumping.

## 7. Dog Walking

![Dog Walking](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kl/z8/pffvb8kq5540eb8918ded944421976_500x500.jpg)

Source: [| We Heart It](https://weheartit.com/entry/173430724/search?context_type=search&amp;context_user=nycteehn&amp;page=3&amp;query=dog+walking)

I don't mean a leisurely stroll around the park - I mean let your big dog pull you through an almost-running half-hour "walk."

## 8. Jump Rope

![Jump Rope](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/rl/on5dsxmg5540eb8a049cc720223706_500x500.jpg)

Source: [Jump rope- best cardio ever](https://weheartit.com/entry/153786141/search?context_type=search&amp;context_user=casandra0801&amp;page=3&amp;query=jump+rope)

It's not just for kiddies on the playground - jumping rope is a great cardio workout!

## 9. Swimming

![Swimming](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/ye/xnh99sh85540eb8ad0015456599098_500x500.jpg)

Source: [| We Heart It](https://weheartit.com/entry/175919819/search?context_type=search&amp;context_user=lifestyle_for_you&amp;query=swimming)

If your joints can't handle a weight-bearing workout (like running), then swimming is for you. It's still a killer cardio workout, but it's gentler on your joints.

## 10. Skating

![Skating](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cc/j5/n92yfr9c5540eb8c945b4190983834_500x484.jpg)

If you're skating on pavement or ice, chances are, your heart's pounding. See? Cardio! You got your workout for the day.

## 11. Jumping Jacks

![Jumping Jacks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/js/c79x8vmy5540eb8c316c1438267364_400x400.jpg)

## 12. Rowing

![Rowing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pu/v2/fdassbae5540ebda05c97824563390_500x500.jpg)

Source: [Untitled](https://weheartit.com/entry/169740897/search?context_type=search&amp;context_user=dianalizeth1802&amp;page=4&amp;query=rowing)

You, a kayak or canoe, and two paddles. Boom. Cardio. Done.

## 13. Zumba/Dance

![Zumba/Dance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b1/y6/ybgxxkby5540eb8f6033d655534617_500x500.jpg)

Source: [Zumba](https://weheartit.com/entry/109133779/search?context_type=search&amp;context_user=noura_mohsen&amp;page=13&amp;query=zumba)

There's a reason Zumba is still so popular - it's fun, yes, but it's such a good cardio workout! Not into Zumba? Try any form of dance, from ballet to tap to just plain clubbing.

## 14. Trapeze

![Trapeze](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ph/ml/sl9izynw5540ebf45568d626339042_500x500.jpg)

## 15. Hiking

![Hiking](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/p0/hfywhzz95540ec0517334057865388_500x500.jpg)

## 16. Tennis

## 17. Volleyball

![Volleyball](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/ta/inj9580r5540eb929eaf0159770221_500x417.jpg)

## 18. Beach Running

![Beach Running](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/st/gq/edn9iz7s5540eb93ad0dd512922145_500x493.jpg)

## 19. Trampoline

## 20. Basketball
