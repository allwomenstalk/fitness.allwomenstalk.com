# 9 Poses from Yoga to Get You Flexible AF ...

According to [PopSugar](https://www.popsugar.co.uk/fitness/Namaskate-Skateboard-Yoga-Craze-38928719), there's a frightening new yoga craze that could end up hurting you. It involves all of the poses you know and love--except this time, a skateboard is involved. You won't want to try these yoga poses unless you're an expert or have some super soft cushions to land on.
